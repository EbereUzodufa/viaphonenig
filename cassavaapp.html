Introduction on CassavaApp

CassavaApp is a business portal application that makes it easy for your customers and/or potential clients to know about your goods and services portal by saving a number. This number can be your phone number or our short-code. The short-codes are between 3 - 5 digits. This number can be saved in phone contact and business portal can view from the mobile app as well as the web. Your portal serves as your website displaying your products and other contacts. Cassava-App is the name of the application because of the uniqueness of Cassava. Cassava from it's leaves to the root saves different purpose. We use the concept of 'Leafs' showcase the various item displays on CassavaApp. Event organizers can use the short-code to invite interested guests on radio stations and other media, this will save most of them the energy of repetition and possibility of mistake which confuses the audience. We have forms to make it easy to carry out documentation and/or event registration; with the scan, you can easily check attendees in. We also have a Q&A leaf to make a free and subscription-based quiz. We created shortcodes like 911 to give easy to access information that can save lives.

FAQ

Cassava is a social media application that addresses the question “why spell-out your contact details to a friend, associate or potential customer when your contact number can do the magic?” With Cassava, all you have to do is download the app from your App store and your prospects phone number. Very easy right? The name “CassavaApp” was chosen because of the versatile use and benefits of Cassava. In fact, statistics have it that Cassava is the third-largest source of carbohydrates and is mostly produced in world by Nigeria. With this fact and Cassava’s unique geometric shape in terms of leaf, bud, seed, and root, it is perfect to name our very unique-multipurpose social media application CassavaApp.
